About Us

Who are we?

At Annie Arkwright's Animal Adoption Organization, our mission is to find loving homes for unwanted animals in New South Wales (NSW). Founded and managed by Annie Arkwright herself, our organization is driven by a deep passion for animal welfare and a commitment to making the adoption process as seamless as possible. We believe in connecting hearts and homes by creating a user-friendly pet rescue website that benefits not only potential adopters but also our dedicated team of volunteers.

Our Objectives

  1. Rescue: Save and protect animals in distress.
  2. Rehome: Find loving homes for our rescued pets.
  3. Educate: Raise awareness about responsible pet ownership.

Our Vision

Bringing Pets and People Together

Our vision is to create a pet rescue website that serves as a bridge between pets in need and compassionate individuals looking to provide them with forever homes. We aim to facilitate the adoption process by harnessing the power of technology and community support.

Our Purpose

To unite pets in need with loving families, creating lasting bonds of happiness and compassion in our community.

Our History: A Lifelong Passion

Annie Arkwright's Adoption Center is the embodiment of Annie's lifelong love for animals. Her journey started in childhood, rescuing strays. Over the years, she volunteered at shelters and saw the need for a dedicated adoption center. With determination and support, the center was born. Today, it thrives as a hub of compassion, connecting animals with loving homes, thanks to Annie's unwavering commitment.
